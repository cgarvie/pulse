Simple, event tracking and alerts for webapps.

(so that you know when things are broken.)

Easily visualize your app's Events, and get emergency alerts when Things appear unusual.

$ pip install pulseapp
$ python
>>> from pulseapp import Pulse
>>> pulse = Pulse('API_KEY')
>>> pulse.event('I made a sandwich', 'ham & cheese')
>>> pulse.event('I sent an email', 'invitation')
>>> pulse.event('attempted website login', 'wrong password')

Tiny bits of code let you visually understand what's happening inside your app.
